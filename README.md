# creative-coding-template
A template students of 197191 can use to format their repositories for submission.

Click the pencil icon to edit text!
Then click "commit changes", then select "Commit directly to the main branch" and click "commit changes" again to save your text.

I te tīmatanga o tēnei haerenga i aro mātou ki te āhuatanga hopu mātauranga a te rorohiko, nā tērā i whakatūwhera i ngā whakaaro kia whai tikanga, kia whai i ngā tohutohu o āku pia, o āku hoa tauira ki te hangaia i tō mātou ake huarahi whakaahua.

Hei whakatakinga kōrero ka uru ki te whare wānanga o P5js, te wāhi e āhuru ai i ngā putanga whaiaro o te tangata, waihoki te ōrokohanga ki tāku whakapuakitanga ki ngā whakaaro whānui e hihiri nei te arotau. 
Ko ngā putanga mahi e toru i kapohia i tēnei wahanga: 

1. Ko te nekehanga o tētahi āhua porowhita e whakamahia ana i ngā reo tauāki (If statements), anōhoki te huringa tae matapōkere hei whakahihiko ai te rerenga, tēnei i tīpako i ngā mahi o te wiki tuatoru. 

2. Ka ruku ki ngā mahinga koromeke whai hua(Loops + For loops), ara, ko tētahi mahi i kapo mai te wiki tuawhā o te wahanga nei he huinga rārangi tapatahi e huri matapōkere ai ngā tae me he poutama, he pākati te āhua hanga.

3. Ka tahuri ki te wiki tuaono, ara, ka kapohia i te putanga tuarua o tērā wiki. He urunga ki ngā akoranga hurihanga (Rotate), he urunga ki ngā akoranga nekehanga (Translate) hoki. I whakauru atu i te āhua porowhita, te āhua tapawhā, me tētahi rārangi mā ka tipu ia te wā ka neke, ka huri porotaka ai ngā āhuahanga i te kānawehi (canvas). I whakauru hoki au i tētahi tohu taituhi anō e whakawātea ai te papa i te wā ka pēhia i te pātene.
